Introduction: Where to go for advice

Policy advice and guidance on civil penalties is given by the Tax Administration, Litigation and Advice (TALA) team (This content has been withheld because of exemptions in the Freedom of Information Act 2000) . They are available to give policy advice on

  • all civil penalties (excluding evasion)
  • excise penalties
  • default interest.
