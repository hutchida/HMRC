About the Financial Products Team

The Financial Products Team in the CT&BIT Product and Process Group includes policy and technical specialists who have responsibility for most of the direct taxation legislation on subjects covered by the Savings and Investment Manual, including interest, the accrued income scheme, deeply discounted securities, collective investment schemes, and annual payments. The Financial Products Team is also responsible for all aspects of the direct taxation rules on corporate finance, including loan relationships and derivative contracts.

Responsibility for legislation on dividends and distributions lies with the Corporation Tax Team.

If you need advice in connection with a marketed financial avoidance scheme you should contact the Anti-Avoidance Group (Investigation).
