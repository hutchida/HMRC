STSM041160:

Exemptions: Charities - Stamp Duty Reserve Tax (SDRT) Exemption

Old version

SDRT exemption for charities

FA86/S90(7) provides a general exemption from Stamp Duty Reserve Tax (SDRT) where securities are transferred to:

  • a charitable company or the trustees of a charitable trust;

  • the Trustees of the National Heritage Memorial Fund;

  • the Historic Buildings and Monuments Commission for England.

    • Details on how to obtain the exemption in CREST can be found at STSM041170.

    There are separate provisions for collective investment schemes subject to the SDRT charge in FA99/SCH19 (see STSM101100).
