PRT: Non-Field Expenditure - Disallowable Expenditure

0

Interaction of Non-Field Expenditure with OTA75\S3(4)

1

Under OTA75\S5(4) certain costs cannot be allowed as allowable abortive exploration expenditure. The provision is applied to exploration and appraisal expenditure by virtue of OTA75\S5A(4).

2

The provision (with appropriate modifications) picks up OTA75\S3(4) (see OT09450) by disallowing

3

interest or any other expenditure incurred in obtaining a loan or any other form of credit,

4

the cost of acquiring any land or interest in land other than the cost of making to the Secretary of State a payment falling within OTA75\S3(1)(b), see OT09075,

5

the cost of acquiring or erecting any building or structure on land,

6

any payment made for the purpose of obtaining a direct or indirect interest in oil won or to be won from any area whatsoever, other than a payment made to the Secretary of State.

7

There is no similar provision for research expenditure in OTA75\S5B; as none of the categories listed above could ever be construed as ‘research’ within the normal English language meaning of the term, there is no need for one.
