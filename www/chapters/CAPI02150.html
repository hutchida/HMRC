Responsibilities Of Rural Payments Agency (RPA)

The RPA is responsible for:

1

applying certain EU CAP Regulations in the UK

2

publishing information to trade bodies about CAP measures

3

issuing import licences

4

providing the EU Commission with information about the granting of licences and certificates, and

5

taking and adjusting licence securities.
