CFM99100

Administration: Penalties: Penalties For Failure To Keep And Preserve Records

0

TIOPA10/SCH7A/PARA39

1

Where a reporting company fails to keep and preserve records as required by TIOPA10/SCH7A/PARA38 CFM98890, it is liable under PARA35 to a penalty not exceeding £3,000. The penalty must be assessed by HMRC within 12 months of the date that HMRC becomes aware of the failure, and the company must be notified.

2

The company may appeal against such a penalty within 30 days.

3

The penalty is payable within 30 days of notification or, if an appeal is made, within 30 days of the appeal being settled or withdrawn.
