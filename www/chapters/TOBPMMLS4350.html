False Or Incomplete Information Provided When Applying For The Licence

0

Where you find evidence on receiving the application that is incomplete or inaccurate with the deliberate intention to mislead, to conceal information, or falsify details, this would lead to a refusal of the application. If discovered once the licence has been granted this will result in a revocation of the licence.
