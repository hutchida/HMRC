Debt buybacks on or after 14 October 2009: CTA09/S361C

1

No deemed release arises under CTA09/S358 on the acquisition of impaired debt by a connected creditor where the equity-for-debt exception applies.

2

Unlike the case with the corporate rescue exception, or the debt-for-debt exception, if the creditor subsequently releases the debtor from the debt, there is no tax charge on a ‘release of relevant rights’ (

3

4

5
