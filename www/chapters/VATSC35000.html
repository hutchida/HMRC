VATSC35000

Payments - The Issue

0

On the face of it, payments of compensation or damages are not consideration for supplies for VAT purposes. This is because they invariably amount to financial settlement of losses caused by breach of agreement or infringement of rights rather than the provision of goods or services. However, there are circumstances when such payments are consideration for taxable services by the recipient of the payments.
