TOBCSC8700:

Restarting The Clock

Old version

0

Following the issuing of a penalty notice or the lifting of an initial notice:

1

the tally of notified seizures for the purposes of the supply chain control system will be reset to zero i.e. will not be considered for any further notices or penalties;

2

a new initial notice may be issued - although only in exceptional circumstances will this be done before a period of six months has elapsed.

3

Any tally of seizures must be reset otherwise the Department may be placed in the position of issuing a penalty twice based on the same seizure data.

4

The resetting of the clock does not prevent further action being taken under existing powers and provisions not relating to the supply chain control system.
