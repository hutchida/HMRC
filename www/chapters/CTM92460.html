CTM92460:

Corporation Tax Self-Assessment Repayment - Intra-Group Surrender - Joint Notice

Old version

0

There is no prescribed form which companies must use to give a joint notice of surrender under CTA10/s963 (formerly FA89/s102). Although the notice can be in any form, it should show:

1

the name and tax reference of the surrendering company

2

the name and tax reference of the recipient company

3

the type and amount of each refund surrendered.

4

An authorised person on behalf of each company (see CTM93170) should sign the notice.

5

The company tax return can be used:

6

by the recipient company to record the amount which has been surrendered to it

7

by the surrendering company to record the amount it is surrendering.

8

Where such entries are made in a return a copy of the joint notice must be included as a PDF attachment. See the CT600 Guide for details of what entries should be made, and where.
