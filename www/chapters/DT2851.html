    Page 2: Admissible taxes

    The following Azerbaijani taxes are admissible for credit in the UK under the agreement:

  • Tax on profit and some incomes of legal persons.

  • Income tax on physical persons.

  • Tax on foreign subcontractors (admissible under Article 2(4)).
