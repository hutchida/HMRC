IPT04330:

Habitually Resident

Old version

An individual is regarded as being habitually resident at the place they give as their current, permanent address on the proposal form. Where this is not in the UK, the insurer should obtain supporting evidence.

Notice IPT1paragraph 5.2.6 contains some useful examples, which may assist you in cases of difficulty.
