title

Registration: Penalties For Failure To Register

0

The Finance Act 1994, Schedule 7, paragraph 14(1) states that a person who fails to register is liable to a penalty of 5% of the tax involved, or £250, whichever is the greater.

1

The Tax Administration Advice Team in Central Policy has policy responsibility for the application of civil penalties, including those in IPT. There is also guidance available in VATCP.
