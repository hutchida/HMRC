CAPI03100:

Other CAP Information Sources

Old version

The following sources may provide useful additional information on CAP imports:

  • the UK Integrated Tariff, particularly Volume 1 part 5, Volume 3 part 3 and the classification nomenclature in Volume 2

  • Departmental guidelines, including Good Practice Guides

  • Public notice 780

  • RPA External Trade (ET) Leaflets and Notices to Traders (NTTs) on their website

  • Tariff Classification

  • CAP liaison officers (CAPLOs)

  • CAP Unit of Expertise (UoE) Intranet site

  • MSS (Management Support System) can provide valuable data about the importer, the product and the nature of the traffic, and

  • DEFRA website.
