COG932140:

Alternative Rights Of Recovery (PAYE Directions): Making A Direction: Direction And Decision Not Signed

Old version

Where the SO authorised officer declines to sign a direction and decision the papers should be returned to you with suitable instructions.

If no remedial action is appropriate and a direction and decision is definitely not to be made you should

  • issue a letter to the employee (and to the agent if appropriate) advising them of the decision not to make a direction and decision

  • close the case as in COG931360 in respect of employees or directors where directions and decisions have not been made.
