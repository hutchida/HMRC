CG78407:

Claims

Old version

Claims under TCGA92/S279, should be submitted to Revenue Policy Business Tax (Exchange). Cases in which it is alleged that remittances have been delayed may be referred to the Inspector by the Collector. If in such cases there are insufficient grounds for discharging the tax, the Collector should be so informed and given an explanation of the circumstances.
