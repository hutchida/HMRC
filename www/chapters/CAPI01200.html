CAPI01200:

Introduction: Objectives

Old version

The principal objectives of the Department’s CAP import arrangements are to:

  • collect agricultural charges on behalf of the EU, whilst maintaining effective control and facilitating legitimate trade

  • ensure that accurate and up-to-date CAP charges are available to officers together with appropriate guidance

  • ensure that the correct CAP charges are levied

  • ensure that valid licences are presented, where appropriate, and are properly processed

  • ensure that amounts charged are promptly and properly brought to account, and

  • ensure that control is effective, while minimising the burdens on trade, and is targeted at areas of greatest risk.
