Preferential Rates Of CAP Duty

EU agreements provide for special preferential rates of CAP duty to be applied to certain goods originating in particular countries or groups of countries.

1

Goods entitled to such rates are displayed in the Tariff and on CHIEF unless they are obtained through the production of a CAP licence.

2

All rates can also be found on the TARIC website.
