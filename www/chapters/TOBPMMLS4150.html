When making a decision you must base it on sound evidence, disclosable at tribunal, that granting a licence would pose a serious threat to the revenue. Examples of evidence that we should consider when assessing the fit and proper status of any key persons would include:

assessments for duty-unpaid or for other under declarations of tax that suggest there is a significant risk that the business would be prepared to trade in illicit tobacco

5

7

8

9

10

previous licences revoked or refused

11
