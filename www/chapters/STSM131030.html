title

CREST: Introduction: Services Provided By CREST

0

The business services CREST provides include:-

1

real time electronic settlement of a range of equity and debt securities, including UK gilts and money market instruments

2

delivery of securities against payment (DVP). See STSM131040.

3

operation of direct links with company registrars enabling registration at the point of settlement for UK stocks

4

straight through processing for UK transaction reporting to regulators (e.g. Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), London Stock Exchange (LSE))

5

fully dematerialised holdings of equity and gilt securities and money market instruments

6

supporting dematerialisation and rematerialisation of securities through its Courier and Sorting Service

7

settling bilateral stock borrowing, lending, collateral management and repo transactions

8

collection of Stamp Duty Reserve Tax on behalf of HMRC (and Stamp Duty on behalf of the Irish Revenue Commissioners).
