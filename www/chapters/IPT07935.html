IPT07935:

Deregistered Claimants

Old version

In some circumstances, insurers that are no longer registered for IPT may ask to operate the scheme. This is acceptable where:

  • the insurer was IPT-registered for the whole period of the claim; and

  • the insurer has the names and addresses of the consumers to be refunded.

    • If there is any doubt whether an insurer can abide by the terms of the scheme, the refund should not be made.
