From 1 August 2018 any person who intends to perform a regulated activity in the UK is required to apply for a licence which gives approval to carry out regulated activities under the scheme.

1

The following activities are classified as a ‘regulated activity’.

2

purchase of an item of tobacco products manufacturing machinery

3

acquire an item of tobacco products manufacturing machinery

4

own an item of tobacco products manufacturing machinery

5

be in possession of any item of tobacco products manufacturing machinery (which also includes leasing a machine).

6

Manufacturing of a tobacco products manufacturing machine, or the machine part(s)
