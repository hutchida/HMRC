PAYE57045:

Dispatch Cover Sheet

Old version

When you have completed or ended a batch the message ‘Dispatch Cover Sheet for Batch No. (unique number) has been printed’ is displayed

  • Select the [OK] button and the Dispatch Cover Sheet is printed

    • The Dispatch Cover Sheet should be placed with the forms P11D (in the same order that they were logged) for that batch and sent to the ‘Receiving Office’.
