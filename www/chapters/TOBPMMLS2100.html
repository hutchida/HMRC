What Is The Relevant Law

0

The laws relating to the Tobacco Products Manufacturing Machinery Licensing Scheme are:

1

The Tobacco Products Duty Act 1979 (TPDA 1979) (as amended by Finance (No2) Act 2017)

2

The Customs and Excise Management Act 1979 (CEMA 1979)

3

The Tobacco Products Manufacturing Machinery (Licensing Scheme) Regulations 2018.
