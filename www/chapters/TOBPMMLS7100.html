Revocation Of Licence

0

A licence may be revoked by us if we find that the following has occurred:

1

a machine is discovered manufacturing tobacco products that are not stated within the licence held.

2

a machine is found at an address that has not been agreed within the licence granting approval.

3

a licensee is found to be involved in illicit activity.

4

a licensee has purchased a tobacco products manufacturing machine from an unlicensed supplier.

5

a licensee has sold a tobacco products manufacturing machine to an unlicensed buyer.

6

a licensee has their registration for producing tobacco products revoked by HMRC.

7

tobacco products from an identifiable tobacco products manufacturing machine is repeatedly found being sold as illicit products.
