CG34773

Settlor Trusts: Cases Where Income Tax Ruling Inapplicable

0

The effect of FA95 was to extend the exceptions in CG34760 to all cases for Income Taxpurposes. It also removed certain transitional rules which protected old trusts from theeffects of Part XV. The test at CG34750(a) is therefore the same for both Income Tax andCapital Gains Tax.
