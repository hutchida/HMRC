PAYE62055:

Work Item 42 - P45 Part 1 - Tax Table Discrepancy

Old version

0

Work item 42 is created when the tax table check, triggered from the processing of leaver information received on a Full Payment Submission (FPS) or P45 Part 1, identifies a discrepancy between the tax code and the pay and tax for the employment on the individual’s record.

1

You should review whether the employer has incorrectly completed the P45 Part 1; if so, offer support and advice on correct procedures to follow.

2

On no account should a duplicate P45 Part 1 be sent in.
