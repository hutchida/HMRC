Rules: Extended Time Limits

0

F(No.2)A17/SCH10/PARAS27-33

1

There are a number of time limits that are extended for the first year of the rules.

2

Members of a group normally have six months from the end of a period of account for the appointment of a reporting company or the revocation of an appointment. This time limit is extended so that an appointment or revocation is valid if made by 31 March 2018.

3

Further, the filing deadline for making an interest restriction return is extended in the first year so that it is not before 30 June 2018.

4

Also, the following elections can be made by 31 March 2018 where they would otherwise be required to be made prior to that date:

5

Election to disapply s484 (which may set a group’s period of account by reference to the single entity accounts of its ultimate parent)

6

Election to specify a period of account

7

Transitional rules for infrastructure

8

Election to be treated as applying the Disregard Regulations

9

The other elections under the rules are typically made by the reporting company in the interest restriction return.
