OT18640:

Risk Assessment - The Risk Review Process

Old version

The Business Risk Review process is described in detail at TCRM3000.

As part of a company’s Business Risk Review the CRM may ask the PRT tax specialist to complete a review from a PRT perspective. Tax specialists should be prepared to discuss the adequacy of the customer’s systems: apart from informing about specific PRT risks, such comments will contribute to the overall assessment of governance and delivery.
