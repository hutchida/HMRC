Capital Allowances: Mineral Extraction Allowance - Limitations On Qualifying Expenditure On Second Hand Assets

There are limits placed on qualifying expenditure on second hand assets under CAA2001\S407-409 (see

1

CAA2001\S410 UK oil licences, the limit is the original licence payment (see

2

CAA2001\S411 Assets generally, the limit is the residue of the previous trader’s qualifying expenditure.

3

CAA2001\S412 Transfer of assets within a group, the limit is the initial group expenditure. A group is defined at CAA2001\S413.

4

There is also a restriction on qualifying expenditure to exclude any expenditure on which a deduction has been given to a previous trader for intangible drilling costs (CAA2001\S407(4)). This covers cases where the previous trader received Case 1 relief under the old New Brunswick treatment.
