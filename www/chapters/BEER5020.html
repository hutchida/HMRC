BEER5020

Conditions Under Which Beer In Duty Suspension May Be Moved

0

All movements of beer in duty suspension are subject to the requirements of The Excise Goods (Holding, Movement and Duty Point) Regulations 2010 (Parts 5, 6, 8, 9, and 10).

1

A guarantee is required in all circumstances for intra-EU movements of duty suspended beer.
