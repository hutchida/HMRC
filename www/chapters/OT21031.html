CTA10\\S282, CTA10\\S285

CTA10\\S282 & S285 cover disposals and appropriations in circumstances where PRT rules do not apply, for example

  • where the company is entitled to oil from a field although not a participator, or

  • where oil is won before determination of a field.

    • The same rules apply as for CTA10\\S280 + in general, with the market value to be taken as the PRT value for the day of delivery or appropriation.
