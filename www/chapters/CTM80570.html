title

Items Eligible For Relief: Potential Restriction On Surrender Of Trading Losses

0

CTA10/S147

1

CTA10/S147 applies where the company owned by the consortium surrenders losses, etc, including a trading loss

2

Under consortium condition 1 to members of the consortium

3

Under consortium condition 2 to companies in the same group as a member of the consortium.

4

The same types of losses, etc, (CTM80110) that can be surrendered on a group claim to group relief can also be surrendered on a consortium claim, however there is a restriction on the amount of trade losses that can be surrendered by a company owned by a consortium.

5

Trade losses can only be surrendered by a company owned by a consortium to the extent that they could not be claimed by that company under CTA10/S37 against its own total profits for that period (CTM04500 onwards), regardless of whether such a set off is claimed or not.

6

See the example at CTM80696.

7

CTA10/S148 may also restrict the amount available for surrender by companies owned by a consortium where that company is also a member of a group relief group. If S147(3) and S148 both apply S147(4) provides that S147(3) is applied before S148.
