Granting A Licence With Conditions

0

Conditions may be placed within the licence giving approval to carry out regulated activities. Some examples (but not exclusive) of conditions that may be put into place are:

1

Tobacco products manufacturing machinery can only be sold to named customers.

2

All sales have to be notified to HMRC

3

Named hauliers only to move tobacco products manufacturing machines.

4

If the licensee does not comply with any conditions attached to the licence we could:-

5

vary the conditions and restrictions of the licence

6

impose a financial penalty

7

revoke the licence
