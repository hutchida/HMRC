Index Of Legislation: Data Protection Act 1998

0

The Data Protection Act 1998 entitles individuals to request access to certain information held on computer records on payment of a fee. The Act

1

Extends only to personal data concerning a living individual

2

Does not extend to partnerships, associations and companies

3

Under the Act you can be held personally liable to prosecution if you

4

Knowingly or recklessly use, obtain, disclose or transfer personal data, otherwise than for the purposes which we have registered

5

Or

6

Disclose personal data without authority
