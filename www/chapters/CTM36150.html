CTM36150:

Investment Of Surplus Funds By The Department For Business, Innovation And Skills

Old version

In certain circumstances the Department for Business, Innovation and Skills (which has responsibility for company law and insolvency) will invest surplus cash balances of a company in liquidation in Government securities. The securities and income from this are placed to the credit of the company (CTM36145).

New version

In certain circumstances the Department for Business, Innovation and Skills (which has responsibility for company law and insolvency) will invest surplus cash balances of a company in liquidation in Government securities. The securities and income from this are placed to the credit of the company (CTM36145).
