1

the sixth anniversary of the end of the accounting period. For example the records for the accounting period ended 31 March 2010 must be kept until 31 March 2016

2

the completion of an enquiry into the matters to which the records relate. For example, if in the case above your enquiry into the return was completed on 14 October 2017 the records must be kept until at least that date, and

3
