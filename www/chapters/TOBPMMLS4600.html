Applicant That Have Previously Traded Without A Licence In The UK

0

Where an applicant has previously been found to have been trading without a licence under the scheme, you must carefully consider the circumstances.

1

As a general rule we would refuse the application if the reason they were performing a regulated activity involving tobacco products manufacturing machinery without a licence was thought serious negligence or for fraudulent purposes.
