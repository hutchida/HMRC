PAYE57901

Introduction

0

Employers are required to complete a form P11D for any director, or employee that they provided benefits in kind to within the tax year.

1

The completed form should be sent to the appropriate HM Revenue & Customs office by 6 July following the end of the relevant tax year.

2

Where forms P11D are not received by the due date, P11D reminders are no longer issued automatically and the facility to print these centrally from ECS has been withdrawn.

3

Where the issue of P11D reminders are required, these must be issued manually using SEES Forms and Letters (PAYE57910).

4

You should not issue reminders in the following circumstances

5

Permanent or temporary inhibition signal is set

6

P11D not due statement has been answered ‘Yes’ on P35 Return

7

A P11D(b) has been received

8

A P11D is not due

9

Employer date commenced is CY or later

10

Employer cessation date is CY-2 or earlier

11

Employer is insolvent

12

Employer is RLS

13

Employer is deceased

14

Employer is dormant

15

Employer is cessation type 1, 2, 3 or 5

16

Employer is cancelled one year only (COYO)

17

Scheme Type 14 - Norwegian Protocol
