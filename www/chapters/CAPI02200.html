Responsibilities Of Her Majesty's Revenue & Customs (HMRC)

This Department is responsible for:

1

accepting import declarations and collecting CAP charges

2

receiving and disseminating information from the Commission about CAP charges (including conversion rates)

3

dealing with claims for exemption from CAP charges

4

ensuring that valid licences and other documents are produced as appropriate

5

ensuring that any other import conditions or obligations are fulfilled

6

verifying - principally by means of examination and sampling - the tariff classification upon which all CAP charges are based, and

7

investigation and prosecution of import offence and fraud cases.
