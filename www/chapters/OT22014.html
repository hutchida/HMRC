title

Charges Which Were Not Allowed Against Ring Fence Profits

0

Before 16 March 2005

1

Charges which could not be allowed against ring fence profits as a result of ICTA88\S494(3) remained allowable against non ring fence profits. An excess of unrelievable charges could be surrendered as group relief.

2

Before 1 April 1998

3

Section 494(4) disapplied the restriction in ICTA88\S403(7). Charges that were not allowed within the ring fence were not debarred from group relief because some ring fence profits remained uncovered.
