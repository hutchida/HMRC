TOBPMMLS2400

Exemptions From The Scheme

0

There are certain exemptions where an individual/company is not required to apply for a licence:

1

if they hold a machine that requires tobacco and paper to be loaded manually into the item

2

the machine makes cigarettes or cigars only by manual operation, and

3

the machine makes only one cigarette or cigar before it requires further loading.

4

A machinery part is exempt from the scheme if it does not meet the criteria described at TOBPMMLS 2350.

5

A licence is not required by any individual/company that is only in procession of the tobacco products manufacturing machine for the sole purpose of transporting it on behalf of a licensee.
