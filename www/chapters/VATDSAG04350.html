Scottish Partnerships

0

Scottish partnership law differs from that in the rest of the United Kingdom. Where

1

you are trying to establish whether there is a single entity, and

2

the businesses are Scottish

3

please read

4

VATREG09050. (External users can find the guidance at http://www.hmrc.gov.uk/manuals/vatregmanual/VATREG09050.htm.)

5

Where a Scottish partnership is properly constituted we would not normally pursue a single entity argument against two or more identically constituted partnerships. However, we may still consider a notice of direction to treat them as a single entity from a current date.
