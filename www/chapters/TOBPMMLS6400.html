Obligations When Purchasing Parts For A Tobacco Products Manufacturing Machine

0

When parts are purchased for the tobacco products manufacturing machine the following details should be recorded and the evidence kept by the licensee:-

1

the unique licence number of the tobacco products manufacturing machine that will be effected

2

the specific identification number that has been assigned to the tobacco products manufacturing machine which requires the part

3

the title of the part concerned

4

details of the part being replaced, including destruction

5
