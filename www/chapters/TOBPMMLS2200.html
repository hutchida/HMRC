TOBPMMLS2200

Who Should Be Approved Under The Scheme

0

When deciding whether to approve an individual or company for a licence under the scheme you should consider:

1

are they established in the UK

2

are they carrying out or intend to carry out a regulated activity (see TOBPMMLS 2050)

3

are there any exemptions that you need to take into account (see TOBPMMLS 2400)

4

do they hold a registration to operate a tobacco factory and store?
