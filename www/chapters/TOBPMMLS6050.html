Obligations For The Individual/Company On Receipt Of The Licence

0

As a condition of the licence the applicant needs to keep records regarding the regulated activity. i.e.:-

1

details of purchase orders or rental details for the machinery

2

sales invoices of any tobacco products manufacturing machine

3

delivery and transportation notes

4

details of any destruction of tobacco products manufacturing machinery

5

sales records including customer ULNs

6

import and export details

7

details of the machines SIN

8

Any changes to the licence must be notified to us before the changes take place to ensure that the applicant is still ‘fit and proper’.
