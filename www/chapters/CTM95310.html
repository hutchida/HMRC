CTSA: Timing

0

FA98/SCH18/PARA36 (2)

1

If you can ascertain the filing date for the return you can make a Revenue determination at any time after that date.

2

If you cannot ascertain the filing date for the return you can only make a Revenue determination after the later of:

3

18 months after the end of the period specified in the notice to deliver,

4

and

5

6

three months after the day of service of the notice.

7

For the normal timing for Revenue determinations see CTM95340and for the circumstances in which you may regard the filing date as ascertainable see CTM95350.
