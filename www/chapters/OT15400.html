title

Receipts - Consideration Received By Connected Persons Under Avoidance Schemes

0

OTA83\Sch2\Para2

1

Tariff and disposal receipts are chargeable if they are received or receivable by a participator in respect of qualifying assets. See OT15025 (tariffs) and OT15060 (disposals). OTA83\SCH2\PARA2 applies where the receipt is not receivable by the participator but

2

by a connected person (see OT15580) in relation to whom the asset is not a qualifying asset and

3

under arrangements the main purpose (or one of the main purposes) of which is the avoidance of PRT or CT.

4

Where this paragraph applies the receipt is treated as a receipt of the participator for the purposes of OTA83\S6 (tariffs) or OTA83\S7 (disposals).

5

As OTA83\Sch2\Para2 does not treat the receipt as that of the participator for the purposes of OTA83\S9 no tariff receipts allowance (see OT15600) may be set against the deemed receipt.
