Tax Treatment Of Sums Paid Out To Meet The Costs Of Decommissioning

0

Relief for decommissioning costs will be due to the company when decommissioning costs are met from monies held by the trust.

1

The reasoning is that, as a matter of tax law, there is no out and out alienation of the monies paid into the fund as the company has a continuing beneficial interest in the fund.
