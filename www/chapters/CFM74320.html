Excess manufactured payments constituting separate fees

13

Where a manufactured payment exceeds the gross amount of the dividends or interest that it represents, the amount of the excess is not treated as manufactured dividends or interest (ITA07/S583 and CTA10/S796-S797). The excess is instead treated for all Taxes Act purposes as a separate fee for entering into the contract or other arrangement under which the payment is made.
