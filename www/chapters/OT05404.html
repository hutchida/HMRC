PRT: Valuation Of Non-Arm's Gas - Valuation Of Light Gases From 1 January 1994 - Buyer’S/Seller’S Nominations

Old version

LB Oil & Gas has no hard and fast rule for valuing the premium of a buyer’s nomination contract or the discount of a seller’s nomination contract. Generally the producers are asked to justify any premiums or discounts that they propose. However LB Oil & Gas takes the view that most, if not all, Southern Basin sales will be on the basis of a buyer’s nomination and will tend to value Southern Basin inter-affiliate sales on this basis even if the contract purports to be a seller’s nomination contract.
