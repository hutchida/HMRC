CTM92600:

CTSA: Amount Due At Each Instalment: Formula

Old version

The amount of tax to be paid at each instalment date is:

3 x CTI n

Where:

  • ‘CTI’ is the amount of the company’s total liability, as described in CTM92520, for the accounting period that is payable by instalments. 

  • ‘n’ is the number of months in the accounting period. Part months are calculated by dividing the number of spare days by 30 and expressing the sum as a decimal to two places, rounded arithmetically.
