ICTA88/S246T and ICTA88/S246V

1

A company was not liable to pay ACT in respect of an FID paid in an accounting period if at the time of payment the company treated itself as an IHC.

2

If the company was found ultimately not to be an IHC in the accounting period, the rule that such a company was not liable to pay ACT in respect of an FID was treated as never having applied. The rules concerning the repayment or set off of ACT in ICTA88/S246N and ICTA88/S246Q (
