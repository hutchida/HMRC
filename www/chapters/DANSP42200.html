DANSP42200:

Of Decision: Notice Of Decision Returned Undelivered

Old version

If a notice of decision is returned undelivered, take action as outlined in DANSP42500 to trace an address.

If an address is traced, print off a new version of the notice. Before issuing the new notice

  • note on it ‘Duplicate of notice issued on [insert date of original notice]’,

  • note on the original notice returned and the DAA1(D) if produced, undelivered ‘Duplicate issued on [insert date duplicate issued]’,

  • file the undelivered notice with your papers,

  • send the duplicate notice with any enclosures sent with the original decision and note your papers.

    • The appeal period, see ARTG2180, runs from the date of issue of the duplicate notice.
