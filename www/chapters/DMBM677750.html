Debt Management Office Actions After Service Of The Summons

0

On the day after the return day you should contact the court and ascertain as to whether or not the defender intends to defend the claim.

1

Defender disputes the claim

2

If he does you should return the form 1 and the certificate of execution of service to the court no later than two days before the date set for the (preliminary) hearing.

3

No response to claim

4

If the defender has not made any response to the claim, you should, no later than two days before the date set for the (preliminary) hearing

5

return the certificate of execution of service to the court and

6

lodge a minute on (Small Claim Court) form 11 seeking decree

7

If you fail to take these actions the case will be dismissed.
