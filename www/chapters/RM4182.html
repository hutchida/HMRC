RM4182:

RO Instructions: R185 Showing Payments To Trade Union/Friendly Society

Old version

After repayment confirm the vouchers as follows

First claims in respect of a new source of income

When an R185 shows a source of income for the first time, send the R185 to the payer’s GCD with a form R36.

All other claims

Every fifth year, send the forms R185 to the payer’s District for confirmation. Send them with a covering form R36.
