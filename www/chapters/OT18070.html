OT18070

PRT: Transfer Of Licence Interests - Exempt Gas

0

FA80\Sch17\Para9

1

For exempt gas generally see OT13200.

2

For the purpose of the calculation required by OTA75\S10(1)(b), to decide if small amounts of oil and gas not sold to the old British Gas Corporation under a pre-July 1975 contract can also be exempt, any transferee (or new participator) is treated as having won the transferor’s (old participator’s) share of oil and gas before the transfer.

3

Such a provision is required because the ‘5% test’ is applied cumulatively.
