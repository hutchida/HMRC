HMAG31650

Revocation Of Approval: The Business Requests Cancellation Of Approval

0

Where a business requests cancellation of its approval you should issue a decision letter confirming cancellation. To cancel the approval you should follow the general steps in this guidance, including providing a notice of revocation of when the cancellation becomes effective and adding conditions to protect the revenue during the notice of revocation period if they are needed.
