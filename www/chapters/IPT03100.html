Purpose And Outline Of This Section

0

This section provides general information about IPT. It identifies the main parts of the legislation that governs IPT and provides assistance by:

1

identifying relevant areas of EU and UK law, to enable you to establish the legal basis for any decisions you make about IPT;

2

giving relevant background information in order to clarify the law and to put it into a practical context; and

3

highlighting areas of possible contention and explaining our current policy in relation to those areas.

4

This section will also help you to decide whether the person you are dealing with is an insurer and whether there is a contract of insurance. When you have decided this, you can go to later sections that deal with liability, registration, valuation and accounting in more detail.

5

Taxes on insurance premiums have existed in one form or another in some other European states since 1850.
