title

Set-Off Of ACT: Accounting Periods Ending On Or Before 5 April 1999

0

ICTA88/S239/PARA2

1

The ACT available for set-off against a company’s liability for an accounting period is the total of any ACT:

2

paid in respect of any distribution made in the accounting period,

3

any surplus carried forward from the previous accounting period under Section 239 (4),

4

any amount surrendered to the company under ICTA88/S240 (1), and

5

any amount carried back under Section 239 (3) from an accounting period beginning before 6 April 1999.

6

The amount of the set-off is limited to an amount equal to the ACT that would have beenpayable in respect of a distribution made at the end of the period of an amount which, together with the ACT thereon, is equal to the company’s CT profit for the accounting period.

7

Guidance on the regime for accounting periods ending before 6 April 1999 can be found at CTM20000 onwards.
