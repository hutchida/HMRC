CTM03540:

No Associated Companies - Profits Exceeding The Lower Limit

Old version

Where in any accounting period of twelve months:

  • a company has no associated companies, and

  • its profits for the accounting period exceed the lower limit but do not exceed the upper limit (for small profits relief),

    • the CT charged at the full rate on its total taxable profits is reduced by an amount of relief equal to:

     

    F x (U - A) x N ÷ A

     

    Where

    F = the standard fraction

    U = the upper limit

    A = the amount of augmented profits of the accounting period (CTM03600)

    N = the amount of taxable total profits (CTM03505).

    CTM03520 refers to the starting rate which applied to financial years 2000 to 2005 and to which a similar marginal calculation applied.
