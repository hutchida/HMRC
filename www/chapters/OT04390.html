If the Responsible Person or a participator wishes to claim expenditure and uplift then it must make a formal claim. Relief for expenditure is obtained by the claimant only after the expenditure is formally allowed on a decision given by the Board. Expenditure allowed on a claim is deducted in computing the assessable profit or allowable loss of a participator in the assessment, or loss determination, for a chargeable period which is next made after the date of the claim decision.
