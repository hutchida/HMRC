IPT07635:

Insurance Premium Tax: Payments In Advance

Old version

Payments made a short time in advance of the tax point under the premium written basis do not affect the tax point.

Example 5

  • Insurer using legal tax point (see IPT07620).

  • Payment actually received on 1 July.

  • Entry made on 10 July.

  • Premium shown as due on 7 July.

  • Tax point - 7 July. In those situations where payments are made more than 14 days before an insurer, using the special accounting scheme creates a tax point the insurer could be affected by arrangements intended to deal with undue delays in bringing tax to account. These are dealt with in

    • IPT07645.
