VATSC64030

Treatment Of Supplies That Fall Within The Scope Of VAT

0

Up until 30 July 2009 supplies of allowances within the scope of VAT were taxed at the standard rate. However, in order to counter fraud certain supplies were zero-rated from 31 July 2009. The zero rate was, in turn, replaced on 1 November 2010 by a reverse charge. For more on the zero-rate and reverse charge see VATSC64240.
