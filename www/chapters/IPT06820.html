title

Legal Basis For The Registration Of Certain Taxable Intermediaries

0

If an intermediary becomes liable to register and account for IPT they will be subject to most of the key obligations and responsibilities that apply to any other IPT registration.

1

Section 53AA(1) of the Finance Act 1994, inserted by the Finance Act 1997, states:

2

A person who -

3

(a) is a taxable intermediary, and

4

(b) is not registered,

5

is liable to be registered.
