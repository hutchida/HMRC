CTA10/Part 14A

1

The transfer of deduction rules were introduced by FA13 for “qualifying changes” of ownership on or after 20 March 2013 (

2

In broad terms the rules prevent deductions where a company changes ownership with expenses that have not yet been recognised for tax, but are ‘highly likely’ (

3

The rules are split into two areas:

4

The loss shifting rules in S730C operate where the deductible amounts would crystallise and be used against a company’s total profits (under CTA10/S37) or be surrendered as group relief (under CTA10/Part 5) (

5

The profit shifting rules in S730D operate where arrangements put profits into a company after a qualifying change and that company has deductible amounts (

6

Both of these are subject to a motive test (

7

The rules were introduced alongside an enhancement to the anti-Capital Allowance buying rules in CAA01/Part 2/Chapter 16A (
