Current practice restricts the checking of declarations to the requirements set out in generated profiles.

1

These requirements must be completed as prescribed in the reason for check contained within the profile.

2

These checks are not fully inclusive and may not cover general risks such as valuation, classification, duty calculations etc.

3

Any other checks would result from post entry assurance activities.
