OT05225:

Manner Of Making A Nomination

Old version

To be effective a nomination must be made:

  • by e-mail to the following address: ian.s.barker@hmrc.gsi.gov.uk or

  • in cases of e-mail disruption, by fax to the following number: 03000 589780.

    • In determining when a nomination has been made the time the e-mail (or fax) was sent will be used (Reg. 3(3)).

    However, a nomination will not be deemed to have been made until it is received by LB Oil & Gas at the relevant address (Reg. 3(3A)).
