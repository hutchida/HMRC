CTM40135:

Clubs: Machine Commissions

Old version

The proprietor of vending or fruit machines, or electronic games, will often pay a commission to a club for the right to install machines on the club’s premises. These commissions are not assessable on the club as trading or miscellaneous income.

 

New version
