Tax Compliance Risk Management Process Guidance

0

In March 2011 the LB published revised Tax Compliance Risk Management Guidance (TCRM) which outlined its approach to compliance in respect of all of tax streams including PRT.

1

Key features are:

2

A New Approach to Governance and Delivery [OT18550 below and TCRM1000]

3

The distinction between Low Risk customers and non Low Risk customers and how HMRC will interact with each category [OT18570 below and TCRM2000]

4

Specific compliance issues: clear errors, voluntary disclosures, [OT18590, TCRM2460]

5

The risk assessment process, [OT18600, OT18610 and TCRM4000]

6

Mandatory work, projects, campaigns [OT18620 and TCRM2450]

7

Regime integrity work [OT18630]

8

The Risk Review Process [OT18640, TCRM3000]

9

The approach outlined in TCRM applies in full to PRT. However because PRT has distinctive features OT18550 to OT18670 will show how it applies to PRT.
