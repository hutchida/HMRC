STSM153010:

Late Stamping

Old version

Stamp Act 1891/S15B makes the general provision for payment of a penalty on the late submission of an instrument for stamping.

A penalty is due if a instrument is presented for stamping more than 30 days after

  • the date the document was executed if it was executed in the UK; or

  • the date the document was first received in the UK if it was executed outside the UK.

    • If an instrument is presented for stamping up to one year after the end of the 30 day period mentioned above, the maximum penalty is £300 or the amount of the duty, whichever is less.

    If a document is presented for stamping more than one year after the end of the 30 day period mentioned above the maximum penalty is £300 or the amount of the duty, whichever is greater.

    All penalties charged are to be rounded down to the nearest multiple of £5.
