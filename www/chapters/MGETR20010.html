MGETR20010

Making A Claim: How To Claim

0

Museums and Galleries Exhibition Tax Relief (MGETR) is a Corporation Tax relief. It is claimed for each relevant accounting period by completing the appropriate section in the production company’s Corporation Tax self-assessment submission (CT600). The company must complete the relevant section titled ‘Information about enhanced expenditure’ and provide supplementary information within their accounts and computations.

1

The supplementary pages to claim MGETR are not available on the simplified Corporation Tax return software.

2

A list of recognised suppliers that provide software for tax returns and supplementary pages is available at Corporation Tax: commercial software suppliers (GOV.UK).

3

Questions about external software should be directed to the software or service provider
