title

What Is An Exchange?

0

An exchange is a marketplace on which stocks and shares, and options and futures on stocks, bonds and commodities etc. are traded.

1

Types of exchanges

2

Most exchanges specialise in a particular type of security, for example:

3

Equities and debt instruments

4

Derivatives, e.g. options, futures, swaps

5

Commodities, e.g. oil, precious metals, grains

6

An exchange provides:

7

liquidity in the market, allowing the trading of securities

8

access for investors centrally to a wide range of securities

9

integrity to the financial markets (due to their regulation)
