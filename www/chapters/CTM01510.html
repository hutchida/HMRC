CTM01510:

Cannot Exceed 12 Months

Old version

0

No accounting period can be longer than twelve months but it may be shorter. For example a company may make up a six month account. There are special rules for certain retail co-operative societies, and the guidance for them is at CTM40555. If a company makes up an account for longer than twelve months, the first twelve months will normally be an accounting period.
