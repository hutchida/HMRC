In the case of R E Newton (LON/2000/0084) VTD 17222, we argued that the appellant was a single entity. The Tribunal ruled that there were two entities of

1

a VAT-registered partnership of father and son, providing carpentry services to VAT-registered customers, and

2

an unregistered partnership of mother and son, providing carpentry services to unregistered customers.

3

To support their claim of separate businesses, the appellants quoted the case of Seabreeze in their appeal.

4

5

6

7

8
