title

CTSA: Introduction: Background

0

CTSA imports the finality and enquiry rules of SA into the general administrativeframework of CTPF. It also brings into a single SA:

1

tax chargeable under ITCA88/S419 on loans or advances by close companies to participators,

2

tax chargeable under ICTA88/S747 on the profits of controlled foreign companies.

3

You should read references in this guidance to ‘tax’ and to ‘CT’ accordingly, where thecontext requires.

4

Provisions in the transfer pricing, Forex and financial instruments legislation requiringthe Board to give a direction before a particular tax treatment could apply are abolished.This is so that they can be integrated within the SA framework.
