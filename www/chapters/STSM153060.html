STSM153060:

Documents Dated In Pencil

Old version

The date of execution of an instrument must be entered in ink or typescript.

HMRC will not assess the duty and penalty upon a document until this has been done. Where the date has been entered in pencil HMRC will return the document and ask that it be inked in.
