HMAG31605

Revocation Of Approval: Overview

0

You should consider revocation of a business’s approval where it is no longer fit and proper to carry out an approved activity, because for example it:

1

is non-compliant, failing to reasonably react to warning letters and/or other sanctions, or failed to notify us that an approval is no longer needed and/or

2

represents a high revenue risk, for example, the business knowingly enters into high risk trading activities.
