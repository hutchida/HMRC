CG99999:

Other Guidance

Old version

There are a number of other sources of guidance produced by HMRC which may be able to assist you. These include:

  • The HMRC website which has a simple guide to capital gains tax (this will move to the gov.uk website).

  • The Capital Gains pages of the SA Return, together with the supporting helpsheets and toolkits.

  • The Venture Capital Manual includes guidance on the Capital Gains aspects of the Enterprise Investment Scheme and on Share Loss Relief.
