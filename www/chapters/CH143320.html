CH143320

Interest: Late Payment Interest: Special Provisions: Special Provisions For Start Dates: Over-Repayment Of Tax

0

You must check whether, and from which date, the FA 2009 interest rules apply to the tax or duty you are dealing with. See CH140160 for full details.

1

Where an amount of income tax or capital gains tax has been repaid to a person who should not have received the repayment, we may assess and recover that amount of tax as if it were unpaid tax under section 30 TMA 1970 .

2

For an assessment under Section 30 TMA 1970, a special provision says that the late payment interest start date is 31 January following the tax year in respect of which the assessment is made.
